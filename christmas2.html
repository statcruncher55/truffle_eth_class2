Christmas Home Tour

Guests at our annual Christmas Home Tour always enjoy our curated collection of pop-up shoppes. Selected vendors have the opportunity to showcase and sell merchandise while simultaneously supporting a life-changing mission. Proceeds provide scholarships for students with signifcant financial need and challenging circumstances. 

A limited number of vendor tables will be available at the Samford President's Home during our 13th annual Christmas Home Tour on Thursday, December 7. Pop-up shoppes will be open 10 a.m. - 2 p.m. and 4 - 8 p.m.

Fee: 20% of pre-tax sales. Minimum fee is $150 and is payable as a deposit upon acceptance as a Pop-up Shoppe vendor. If the vendor is approved as a Pop-up Shoppe vendor, the deposit will be non-refundable. The deposit will be credited against the 20% fee, and the balance shall be due upon the conclusion of the event. Upon acceptance, vendors will be invoiced for their deposit. 
 

  • Vendors will be provided one 6-foot table with black table covering and two chairs.
  • Internet access will not be provided; if needed, vendors shall provide their own hotspot. 
  • The Legacy League will attempt to provide one electrical outlet to each vendor who requests one. Extension cords will not be provided.
  • Vendors are solely responsible for having all necessary and appropriate business licenses and paying all sales taxes attributable to merchandise sold by vendor. The Legacy League reserves the right to prohibit the sale of any merchandise that it determines to be inconsistent with the Christian mission and values of Samford University.

Each Pop-up Shoppe will be conducted in accordance with then-applicable state and local laws, ordinances and orders governing public health including, but not limited to, those regarding Coronavirus. By submission of the completed vendors application, the undersigned business agrees to comply with all such requirements. Applicant acknowledges, approves and accepts the foregoing conditions.

Quality of merchandise and vendor variety will be considered in the selection of vendors.

Upon acceptance, vendors will be invoiced for Deposit. The Deposit must be remitted within 10 days of invoice to confirm participation.
